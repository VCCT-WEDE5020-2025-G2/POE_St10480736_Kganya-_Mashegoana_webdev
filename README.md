## Thando Kitchen Website 

# Overview 

Thando’s Kitchen, founded in October 2021, is a small family-run catering business. It provides catering for weddings, birthdays, funerals and more.
The goals the business wants to achieve:
-	To Increase User visibility of their business online
-	To be able to provide customers with the ability to book their services using their website loctaed in the contact details 
-	Enabling user engagement by means of reviews and feedback and enquires 

## purpose 
 - The website is to provide an online recognition for the business
 - To provide users the ability to make  bookings 
 - To be able to contact the business and enquire
 - also to Enable users Engagement in the website
 - display their products
 - give a set list of what services they provide with their costs
 - User friendly navigation

- Gallery Page

- -Enquireing Page
  A page for the user to enquire in the business if they want to book the business services
  - Contact Page
